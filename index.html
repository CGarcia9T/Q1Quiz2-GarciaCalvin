WALL•E

WALL•E is a 2008 animated romantic science fiction film produced by Pixar, an animation studio for Walt Disney Pictures. After the making of "Finding Nemo", Andrew Stanton, the director of WALL•E, wanted to create a film set in space. The film has minimal dialogue and mainly uses visuals to communicate its meaning. Waste Allocation Load Lifter Earth-Class or WALL•E is a robot that collects trash on planet Earth until one day, he boards a ship and meets a new robot.
